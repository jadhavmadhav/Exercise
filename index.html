Introducing

Exercise is physical activity that is planned, structured, and repetitive for the purpose of conditioning the body. Exercise consists of cardiovascular conditioning, strength and resistance training, and flexibility.

Top 5 Exercise

Yoga

Yoga originated in India and well famous around the world for different techniques of yoga fitness. Yoga asanas, pose and exercises improve flexibility of body and mind and can be practiced by any people young and old alike. Few basic yoga postures are Bal Asana, Sukh Asana, Kapalabhati and the best morning Yoga stretching poses – Surya Namaskar. Daily practice of Yoga Asana’s leads to better life, great energy and improve your health and fitness.

Walking

Walking is the gentle,easy and low-impact exercise that can bring you into a high level of health and fitness. Like other exercise, walking can help you to achieve so many health benefits like lower your blood pressure, manage your weight and make you happy strong and fit. Include the stretching and cool down technique with walking for flexibility of body muscles and to reduce stress.Be stylish and look beautiful every day does not made you strong, Even eating hygiene food and maintain a proper diet plan is also not sufficient to stay fit and healthy in today’s lifestyle. Daily workout is must for every one to remain fit and fine, Exercise is one of the best way to gain energy and feel better for a long life. There are so many day to day exercise which can be done at home or around such as dance, walking, play a sport,use the stairs or learn the various breath control, meditation and relaxation techniques. Add these daily exercise early in the morning into your busy schedule followed by a balanced and healthy diet as well as some effective fat burners for men to stay happy and healthy.

Jogging

Jogging is running slowly with less stress on the body than from faster running. Jogging is one of the easiest ways to start getting fit and healthy, It increase aerobic and cardiovascular fitness. Running and Jogging help to get you in shape, improve energy levels, promote weight loss and stay fit & strong.

Swimming

Swimming is such as simple yet effective form of exercise and the best fitness choice for just about everyone. Swimming is a full-body workout and a lifetime sport that benefits the body and the whole person, especially those who have physical limitations. It is a good cardiovascular and muscle workout and best for the people who need to build fitness. Swimming is one of the popular sport with good fitness benefits like calories burned, strokes – freestyle, backstroke, butterfly and breaststroke.

Play Any Sports

Sports are best for the whole body fitness, specially to support performance, lean muscle building, toning and fat loss. Sports such as badminton, basketball and tennis are the best form of exercise and fitness, Also sports make you feel happy and energetic.
